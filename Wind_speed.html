Wind Speed

From our list of 500 cities, we query the OpenWeatherMap API for the wind speed along with the latitude. The responses are taken from JSON and appended onto list. Next we create a dataframe to hold these list and use MatPlotLib to generate a scatter plot of latitude vs. wind speed.

Looking at the visualization, it appears that the closer we get to the north pole, the higher the wind speed becomes.
